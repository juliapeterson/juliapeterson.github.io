Meet Jules

Jules, a 21 year old nursing student attending Villanova University, has quickly established herself as a household name in live entertainment on the Cape. Since high school, she can be found jamming at locations such as The Pilot House and The Bog, opening for bands at the XFinity Center, and completing private events. If you want to make a night one to remember you are going to want the talent of Julia Peterson.
